This demo was made as part of a project that arose from a COVID-19 hackathon: Miia, a two way app platform for elderly and caregivers.

Note that AI models are not 100% accurate. Please consult your doctor for proper medical analysis if you have any concerns about the results above.

The emotion prediction model was trained on publically available images of faces of famous people. For best results, the face should be forward facing and centered in the image. The BMI prediction model was adapted from a model trained from a private dataset.
